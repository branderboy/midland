Insight 2 · Why Leads Actually Contact You 4 triggers, learned from the business

The #1 reason people book. Spill, flood, last-minute event, damaged floor before move-in. Needs same-day response.

3. Referral / Upper-Mgmt Visit

Faster turnaround, often triggered by a property visit from upper management or an inspection. Hot lead, expedited quote.

Inquiry for a service Midland doesn't provide. Logged, politely declined, optionally referred, but never lost in the inbox.
