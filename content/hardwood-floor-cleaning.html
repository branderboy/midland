Frequently Asked Questions

How often should hardwood floors be professionally cleaned?

For most homes, once or twice a year. High-traffic commercial spaces benefit from a recurring monthly or quarterly maintenance plan to protect the finish and keep floors presentable.

Will professional cleaning damage my wood floor's finish?

No. We identify your finish first and use pH-balanced, wood-safe products and the correct equipment — the opposite of harsh store-bought cleaners or rented machines that can dull or strip the surface.

Do you clean both residential and commercial hardwood floors?

Yes. We service homes as well as offices, medical buildings, retail, and shopping centers across DC, Maryland, and Northern Virginia.

What's the difference between cleaning and refinishing?

Cleaning restores the look of a sound floor by removing dirt and residue. Refinishing repairs the surface itself (sanding/recoating) when a floor is scratched, worn, or has lost its protective layer. We offer both and will tell you honestly which your floor needs.
