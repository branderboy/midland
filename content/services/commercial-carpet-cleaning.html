Frequently Asked Questions

How often should commercial carpet be professionally cleaned?

Most offices benefit from quarterly cleaning, while high traffic lobbies and retail spaces often need monthly service. We will recommend a schedule based on your foot traffic.

Can you clean after business hours?

Yes. Most of our commercial work is done after hours or on weekends so your carpet is dry and ready before your team returns.

How long does carpet take to dry?

Low moisture encapsulation dries in about one to two hours. Hot water extraction usually dries within a few hours with proper airflow.
