Tile and Grout Cleaning That Reaches the Grout Lines

Grout is porous, so it traps dirt, grease, and moisture that regular mopping cannot reach. We use high pressure cleaning and specialized solutions to lift the buildup out of the grout lines and off the tile surface, then we can seal the grout to keep it cleaner longer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you make my old grout look new?

In most cases yes. Deep cleaning removes years of buildup, and sealing keeps it looking better longer. For grout that is damaged, we will recommend the best option.

Do you seal grout after cleaning?

We can. Sealing is the best way to protect freshly cleaned grout from staining again.

Is this safe for natural stone?

Yes. We adjust our products and pressure for ceramic, porcelain, and natural stone.
