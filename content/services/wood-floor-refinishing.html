Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my floor needs refinishing instead of cleaning?

If the finish is worn through, the wood is gray or stained, or scratches reach the wood itself, refinishing is the right fix. We will tell you honestly which your floor needs.

How long does refinishing take?

Most projects take a few days depending on size and the finish used. We will give you a clear timeline up front.

Can I choose a different stain color?

Yes. Refinishing is a great time to change the color of your floors to match your space.
