Frequently Asked Questions

How often should hardwood floors be professionally cleaned?

Most homes do well with one or two professional cleanings a year. High traffic commercial spaces benefit from a recurring maintenance plan.

Will professional cleaning damage my floor's finish?

No. We identify your finish and use pH balanced, wood safe products, the opposite of harsh cleaners that dull or strip the surface.

What is the difference between cleaning and refinishing?

Cleaning restores the look of a sound floor. Refinishing repairs the surface itself when a floor is worn or scratched. We offer both and will tell you honestly which yours needs.
